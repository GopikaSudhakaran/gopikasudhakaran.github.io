I am a PhD student at TU Darmstadt (advisors: Prof. Stefan Roth, Prof. Kristian Kersting) specializing in Structured Reasoning, Neuro-Symbolic AI, and Vision-Language Models.

Targeting robust OOD generalization, I extend structured scene understanding to embodied agents—integrating open-vocabulary perception with logic to rescue frozen RL agents and benchmark ego-centric reasoning. My work has been published at top-tier venues including ICCV and NeurIPS.

Current Research & Projects

ThinkRL: Neuro-Symbolic Cognitive Co-Pilot

A modular framework to rescue frozen RL agents in OOD scenarios using open-vocabulary perception and logic. Overcomes the closed-world assumption of prior neuro-symbolic methods by grounding novel objects zero-shot.

Benchmarking Faithfulness in VLM Reasoning

Investigating "Clever Hans" effects in Vision-Language Models using a custom ego-centric reasoning benchmark to distinguish genuine spatial reasoning from statistical pattern matching.
